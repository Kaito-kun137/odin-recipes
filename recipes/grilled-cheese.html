Pickle Grilled Cheese Recipe

Description

A delicious and easy-to-make grilled cheese sandwich with the addition of pickles for a tangy twist.

Ingredients

  • 8 slices of sourdough bread
  • 3 tablespoons of mayonnaise
  • 4 slices of cheddar cheese
  • 4 slices of Swiss cheese
  • 1/4 cup of dill pickle slices
  • Butter for spreading on bread

Instructions

  1. Preheat a skillet over medium heat.
  2. Spread butter on one side of each slice of bread.
  3. On the unbuttered side, spread mayonnaise on 4 slices of bread.
  4. Add a slice of cheddar cheese, a slice of Swiss cheese, and a few pickle slices on top of the mayonnaise.
  5. Top with the remaining slices of bread, buttered side out.
  6. Place the sandwiches in the preheated skillet and cook until golden brown, about 3-4 minutes per side, or until the cheese is melted.
  7. Remove from skillet, slice in half, and serve warm.
